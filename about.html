Our Story

Built in Detroit.
Built for the game.

We're two guys who got tired of confidence being sold as a self-help course. So we built a game instead.

Why We Built AuraBet

Most confidence apps feel like homework. Flashcards, journaling prompts, breathing exercises — all great in theory, but nobody actually uses them when the moment hits.

We wanted something different. Something that felt like pulling out your phone to play a game, not do therapy. AuraBet turns the anxiety of making a move into the thrill of placing a bet — because confidence shouldn't feel like work, it should feel like winning.

We're just getting started. The goal is simple: help every guy who's ever hesitated feel like the highest roller in the room.
